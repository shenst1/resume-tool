# SWOT — Orbis (Product Front-End, confidential client)

## Strengths

- **Recruiter** can streamline intro and salary conversation if they are engaged and credible.
- **Role** reads like real **ownership** (core UI, founders)—good fit if the client matches the pitch.

## Weaknesses

- **Client anonymity** until late stage—you learn less from public research than a direct company post.
- Recruiter copy can **overstate** traction, logos, or comp; verify with diligence.

## Opportunities

- If the client is genuinely **early** and **well-backed**, equity and scope can be attractive.
- **US remote** broadens fit.

## Threats

- **Time cost** if the role is already filled or the client narrows on a different profile.
- Misalignment on **stack** or **seniority** until you speak with the hiring team.

## Notes

- Ask Orbis for **company name**, **stack**, **interview loop**, and **comp range** early.
- Confirm whether you are employed by **client** or a **staffing** arrangement (likely client W-2).
