# SWOT — Corelight (Senior UI Engineer, Fleet Manager)

- **Clear category** in **NDR** with strong **open-source** lineage (**Zeek** ecosystem)—credible story for buyers who care about evidence.
- **Problem** (network evidence, fleet operations) rewards **great ops UX**; a strong UI hire can materially reduce time-to-triage.
- **Investor and analyst** visibility in **security** can help recruiting and retention.

## Weaknesses

- **Buyer** is often **sophisticated** and **risk-averse**; product and UI must survive **scrutiny** and **long evaluations**.
- **Data scale** and **latency** expectations can stress frontend architecture—technical debt shows up fast.
- **Compensation** band may or may not match **Bay Area** anchors depending on level and location.

## Opportunities

- **AI-assisted** workflows are a differentiator theme in company narrative—room to improve **operator** flows if executed carefully.
- **Fleet** and **investigation** surfaces may share patterns; good UX systems work can lift multiple products.

## Threats

- **Crowded** security market; incumbents bundle **NDR-like** capabilities.
- **Talent competition** for senior **React** engineers who tolerate security-domain complexity.
- **Burnout** risk if on-call or incident culture is heavy—verify expectations.
