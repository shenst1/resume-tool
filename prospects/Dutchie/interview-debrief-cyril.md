## What to improve

### Staff framing (main gap)

Cyril asked **Staff vs Senior** and **how Plantfolio prepares you for Staff at Dutchie.**

| You said (summary) | Stronger pattern |
|--------------------|------------------|
| Staff = multiply engineers, standards, mentor | Same definition is fine for **10s** |
| Plantfolio = solo learning, cutting edge, no manager blocking experiments | See **Better Plantfolio angle** below; then **Bloom/Tilia proof** for multiplying others |
| “Big learning spree” | Product/customer judgment + **team impact** at Bloom/Tilia |

### Better Plantfolio angle (Staff — honest about scale)

You did **not** influence other engineers at scale at Plantfolio (solo founder). Don’t claim that. **Do** claim Staff-shaped work you actually did:

- **Customer discovery** — conferences, garden centers, pivot from consumer app → B2B signage when you heard the real job (“weekend of signs”).
- **Product empathy** — talking to buyers/operators, not only building; understood sales/partner gap and exit criteria with your spouse.
- **Navigating client-shaped requests** — when an ask sounds like a one-off, ask what job the feature is hired to do; scope what generalizes vs custom (same muscle as helping ICs handle PM/enterprise pressure).

That maps to what Cyril probed (**pushback on product**, stakeholder stories) better than “I learned cutting-edge tech alone.” Technical learning at Plantfolio is still great for **senior IC / AI-native** roles; for **Staff**, lead with **judgment under product and sales pressure**, then hand off to Bloom for **team-scale** proof.

**Weak:** “I care about product and talked to customers.”

**Strong:**

> At Plantfolio I was product and engineering — discovery with garden centers, scoped the B2B pivot from real pain, and pushed back on one-off scope when a generalized solution fit. I didn’t have a team to multiply; that’s why I want Staff work **with** engineers and PMs. At Bloom I did the same with product on big-client asks — embedded Shopify was the ideal UX but not shippable; we reframed to standalone Next, auth POC first. That’s the Staff work I want: help engineers and PMs get to the platform answer, not the loudest customer’s first draft.

**Staff 60s template (revised):**

> Staff means multiplying good technical decisions — often at the product boundary. At Plantfolio I owned discovery and scoping with real customers; at Bloom I reframed enterprise asks and led the standalone Next bet with the team; at Tilia I helped drive Chakra and test discipline. I’m honest I didn’t coach a large eng org solo — my proof at scale is Bloom/Tilia. At Dutchie I’d focus on one wedge — POS/menu source-of-truth or GraphQL/Mongo standards — and help ICs and PMs land the generalized solution.
