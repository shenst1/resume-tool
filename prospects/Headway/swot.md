- Mission with clear societal pull: access to therapy via insurance and provider tooling.
- Very relevant stack on paper (TypeScript, Python, React, Next.js/Remix, Postgres, modern infra).
- Room for high impact on trustworthy AI: eval loops, observability, clinical safety—exactly the hard part of shipping LLMs in regulated space.

- Payers & Outcomes sits at the intersection of ML, product, and trust—good place to grow a portfolio of “serious” AI systems, not chat demos.

## Threats

- Competitive hiring and mission-driven burnout if product velocity and ethics expectations stay intense.
- Regulatory and reputational risk if AI-assisted clinical or claims features miss the trust bar (industry-wide concern, not Headway-specific).
