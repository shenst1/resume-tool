## Top priorities (from debriefs — work these first)

### 1. Staff answers need proof, not definitions

When the role is **Staff** (or staff-shaped IC):

1. **10s** — What Staff means here (multiply engineers, standards, cross-team outcomes).
2. **30s** — **Proof:** Bloom / Tilia for multiplying others (Amir, standalone Next, Chakra/tests, Tune POC, PR bar). **Plantfolio** = product empathy, customer discovery, scoping client-shaped asks toward generalized solutions — **not** “I influenced engineers at scale” (solo founder; stay honest).
3. **20s** — **At {company} I’d…** — one wedge (e.g. caching layers, GraphQL/Mongo boundaries, design system, review tooling).

**Avoid:** Leading with “I got to try cutting-edge tech alone” as your Staff prep story. **Prefer:** helping engineers and PMs navigate product/sales pressure → platform answer (see [Dutchie debrief — Better Plantfolio angle](prospects/Dutchie/interview-debrief-cyril.md#better-plantfolio-angle-staff--honest-about-scale)).

### 2. Last ~15 minutes: rule of three

- Prepare **three** questions max; go deep on one if they invite it.
- End with a **tight close** (30s): why this company, why this level, why now.
- **Email the architecture link** (or doc) after the call — don’t rely on in-meeting paste (nofollow / chat links fail).
- If time is up: “I have one more question — happy to send the rest by email.”

### 3. Speech and presence (especially when tired)

- **Pause** instead of “um / you know / I guess.”
- **One idea per breath**; stop when the beat is done.
- Record 5-minute answers to Staff / pushback / proudest project; listen once for fillers.
- Shorter answers beat long rambles when energy is low.

### 4. Preload three stories before every interview

| Story | What they’re testing |
|-------|----------------------|
| **Pushback** | IC reframed product ask for big customer → better outcome for many (not “we compromised”) |
| **Platform / Staff** | Decision that changed how **others** ship (standards, migration, tooling) |
| **Regulated / high stakes** | Bloom or Tilia — compliance, money, or trust (maps to fintech, commerce, health) |

### 5. AI and velocity (large companies)

Lead with **guardrails**, then speed. See [interview-reminders.md](prospects/interview-reminders.md).
